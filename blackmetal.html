What exactly is black metal?

Black metal is a genre of metal characterized by raw vocals, no real song structure, fast tempos, and poor recording quality (this is no joke). You may hear that black metal is poorly produced, and I will have to say, that yes, it kind of actually is. There are many subgenres of black metal, but for now I will be telling you about first-wave, second-wave, and atmospheric black metal. First-wave bands consist of Bathory, Burzum, and Mercylful Fate. Burzum, or more specifically, Varg Vikernes, is known as the father of black metal. So, what are some good starter albums/songs?

Bathory's self-titled album is an excellent place to start. It's more of experimental metal, but I still consider it black metal. It was one of my first bm albums and I absolutely love the goat on the cover. By the way, don't be mistaken--it says Bathory, not Batlord.

Mayhem's Deathcrush is also an excellent place to start.
